What affects quote options?

Long-distance moving quotes depend on more than mileage.

Mileage matters, but it is only one part of a long-distance move. Shipment size, route demand, pickup access, delivery access, packing, storage, dates, and availability can all affect quote options.

Pickup and delivery access

Apartment walk-ups, elevators, parking limits, gated communities, long carries, and building restrictions can change the moving plan.

Service needs

Packing, storage, fragile items, large items, disassembly, or special handling may affect availability and pricing.

Dates and flexibility

Month-end dates, summer moves, short notice requests, and tight delivery windows can affect which options may be available.

FAQ

Questions about how ModelMoving works.

These answers explain the basics of the ModelMoving quote request process.

Is ModelMoving a moving company?

ModelMoving is a moving quote request and matching platform. It helps organize customer move details and may connect customers with selected moving partners.

Is submitting a request free?

Yes. Submitting a moving quote request through ModelMoving is free and there is no obligation to purchase services.

What information should I include?

Helpful details include pickup city or ZIP, delivery city or ZIP, move date or window, home size, inventory, packing needs, storage needs, stairs, elevators, parking access, and special items.

Will I receive a guaranteed price immediately?

No. A request helps organize your information. Final pricing depends on move details, service availability, route, shipment size, access, timing, and provider pricing.

Can I use ModelMoving for specialty routes?

Yes. ModelMoving includes support pages and route pages for state-to-state moves and specialty routes such as Hawaii, Alaska, and Puerto Rico.
