Access details

Boston pickup details

  • Downtown Boston parking and loading access
  • Elevator reservations and building time windows
  • Certificate of Insurance requests for condos or managed buildings
  • Walk-ups, tight streets, and long carries
  • Storage pickup timing around Greater Boston

Florida delivery details

  • Florida condo elevator reservations
  • Gated community and HOA delivery rules
  • Long walk or shuttle situations
  • Storage-in-transit before final delivery
  • Seasonal timing for summer and snowbird moves

What to include in your notes

List large furniture, boxes, packing needs, storage, stairs, elevators, long walks, parking limits, building rules, and any date flexibility.
