Seasonal planning

August and September Hawaii moves should be planned early.

Late-summer and early-fall island moves can involve tighter shipping schedules, storage coordination, vehicle-shipping timing, and less flexibility around fixed move dates.

  • Submit the request early when pickup or delivery dates are firm.
  • Include flexibility if your shipping or delivery window can move.
  • Mention vehicle shipping and storage needs from the start.
  • List condo rules, elevators, parking limits, and special items clearly.
